<div style="text-align: justify;">

<p>
Co-authored a long-term urban documentation project focused on identifying and mapping historical street name plaques in Santiago, Chile.
</p>

<p>
Over more than a decade, the project involved systematic field data collection, geolocation, and classification of surviving and missing plaques across the city.
</p>

<p>
The resulting dataset provides a structured view of urban cultural heritage, highlighting patterns of preservation, loss, and transformation in the city landscape.
</p>
