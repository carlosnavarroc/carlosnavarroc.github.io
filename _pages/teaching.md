- **Positions:** Assistant Professor (Universidad Santo Tomás, Universidad Andrés Bello); Lecturer (Universidad de Chile, Universidad del Desarrollo)  
- **Duration:** 2015 – Present
- **Overview:** Teaching is one of my core professional interests. Coming from a family of educators, I deeply value engaging students in the learning process, fostering both foundational knowledge and applied skills.  
I have taught courses ranging from mathematics and physics to specialized topics in electrical engineering, including [image processing](https://tinyurl.com/Clases-CNC-Imagenes) and [laboratory work](https://www.die.cl/academicos-jornada-parcial/), emphasizing hands-on experience, problem-solving, and computational thinking.

## Teaching Philosophy and Focus

- Emphasizes **hands-on learning**, critical thinking, and real-world applications of computational methods  
- Integrates **machine learning, computer vision, and AI** into engineering curricula  
- Mentors students to **publish, present, and implement** their work in academic and applied settings  
- Encourages reproducibility, ethical AI, and open-source contributions in student projects  
- Supports **interdisciplinary collaboration** across engineering, medical technology, and physical sciences
