<div style="text-align: justify;">

<p>
Co-developed <strong>VolumePeeler</strong>, a software tool for advanced visualization and analysis of 3D biomedical image data, implemented as a FIJI plugin.
</p>

<p>
The method enables virtual "peeling" of volumetric datasets using spherical and spline-based surface projections, allowing structured exploration of complex biological geometries.
</p>

<p>
The tool improves both <strong>visual interpretation and quantitative analysis</strong> of microscopy data, supporting research workflows involving 3D imaging of tissues and embryos.
</p>
