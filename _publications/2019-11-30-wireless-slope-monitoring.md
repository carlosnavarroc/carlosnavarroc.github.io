<div style="text-align: justify;">

<p>
Contributed to the design and implementation of a wireless sensing platform for monitoring ground deformation and pore pressure in open-pit mining environments.
</p>

<p>
The system integrates control units, power systems, and Bluetooth-based telemetry, supporting both laboratory validation and field deployment.
</p>

<p>
This work demonstrates the development of <strong>end-to-end sensing infrastructure</strong> for geotechnical monitoring under real-world conditions.
</p>
